      9 I like listening to podcasts. I like to listen them on a dedicated podcast player. Dedicated podcast player means support for chapter markers, custom thumbnail, clickable timestamp on shownotes, etc.
     10 
     11 Some of my favorite shows moved to be Spotify exclusives. I don't like Spotify as podcast player. I do like Spotify as music player.
     12 
     13 [spotifeed.timdorr.com](https://spotifeed.timdorr.com) turns Spotify exclusive podcasts to a regular RSS feed for your favorite PODCAST player.
