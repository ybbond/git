     11 I do— We do
     12 We all do commute
     13 Like vast herd of sheep
     14 To works, schools, shops
     15 Like migrating ducks
     16 with hunters below
     17 Nozzle aimed
     18 Ready to shoot
     19 We all do commute
     20 
     21 <br>
     22 
     23 Don't you all bored?
     24 With this routinity?
     25 Mornings—Nights
     26 Even the sun—moon mock;
     27 ironically
     28 They do commute
     29 too
     30 
     31 <br>
     32 
     33 Accompany
     34 human commuters
     35 "keep cycling the city,
     36 you humans"
     37 "you too moon, to the sun"
     38 "you too sun, to the galaxy"
     39 We all do commute
     40 Became our comfort zone
     41 How else would we get salary,
     42 or study,
     43 or resource for food?
     44 
     45 <br>
     46 
     47 We need to commute
     48 Gaining stuffs while
     49 keep cycling the route
     50 
     51 <br>
     52 
     53 "No I dont need to,
     54 I already get all things"
     55 "Yes, yes, lazy ass!
     56 While your relatives do
     57 the commute,
     58 you sleeps, eats, mates.
     59 Let's see how far your life goes
     60 without doing it yourselves
     61 Because
     62 We need to
     63 do
     64 the Commute."
     65 
     66 <br>
